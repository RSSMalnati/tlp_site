The TEP Experience

As a TEP, you're a part of a network that includes dozens of new friends, mentors, and colleagues from Day 1. Your role will keep you challenged, and our program culture includes more components to help you grow your skills, expand your horizons and have fun.

Supportive

TEPs (TEPle, Teeps - there's kind of a debate) have each other's backs. When you join, they'll help you learn the ropes, and you'll help lift up the new hires that come after you.

Collaborative

Participate in our regular series of hackathons and tech talks, or come up with your own to host! We're all about sharing knowledge and learning together, and all ideas are welcome.

Social

Whether you need a workout buddy, a crew for a concert, a group for a board game night, or anything else - there's always a group up to something. You'll have an overwhelming number of new friends!
