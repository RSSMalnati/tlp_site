Program Overview

An effective STEP 2 internship should help fill our your resume and prepare you for what’s next. Our goal is to expose you to as many teams and people as we can, so you can pick up new skills and find out what types of roles you’re most interested in for the future.

After a successful STEP 2 internship, you can qualify for one of our full-time programs and positions for after graduation: either the Technology Engineering Program (TEP) or the Technology Leadership Program (TLP). Enjoy frequent networking and social events to meet other interns, full-time employees and managers.

Program runs June 3-August 9, 40 hours per week, with working hours roughly from 9-5. This is a paid internship, with a relaxed dress code (jeans are fine) and work envirinment (you can play ping-pong, and becoming friends with your coworkers is encouraged)

Possible Roles

Data Science

Provide the insights that help us make the best decisions for our customers and ensure we’re operating at the highest levels of efficiency. Build out our big data architecture, work with analytics tools, and develop new capabilities.

Platform Engineering

Drive our software architecture, our technology roadmap, and our relationships with enterprise partners. Empower our workforce by providing the tools and platforms that help us reach our goals, and work with cloud technologies.

Technical Product Management

Own the technical vision for products tied to a customer journey. Work with product owners to transform business requirements and customer experiences into tech-ready user stories. Collaborate with a team of engineers to bring the platform product roadmap to life.

Information Security

Keep our customers’ money safe. Help ensure our products meet security standards by embedding yourself into a project or business as an Information Security Officer or in Information Security Architecture.

Development

We've got it all, whether it's building features for Citi Mobile on iOS (Swift & X-Code) or Android (Android Studio), creating something new for Citibank Online (Angular), engineering a new microservice (Java, Spring), or working on the back-end systems that power one of the world's biggest financial instituations (Java, SQL, COBOL). We prefer our engineers to be well-rounded, so you'll likely have an opportunity to work on more than one of these.

Other Roles

Scrum Master, Software Architect, Project Manager, Risk Control, Finance, Demand Management, API Catalog Management, Robotics (role available in Mexico City only), and more!

Your Summer Experience

There are three main components of STEP 2. Each of them is meant to expand your skill set, add to your resume, and introduce you to some great people.

Summer Role

You’ll be placed into a team just like a full-time employee - this isn’t a “hey go make us coffee” situation. Around 80 percent of your time goes here.

Side Project

Work with other interns on…whatever you want! this is a super flexible way to spend 20 percent of your time. Choose one of our ideas or create your own.

Intern Project

Toward the end of the summer, we get all the interns together at one of our offices for a few days of special events, skill development and socializing.
